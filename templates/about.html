About Peer Tutoring Platform

The Peer Tutoring Platform is a web-based application designed to facilitate academic support through a structured peer tutoring system. Our platform connects students with tutors, enabling them to schedule sessions, communicate effectively, and track progress. Whether you're a student seeking help or a tutor looking to share your knowledge, our platform is built to make learning collaborative, accessible, and effective.

User Roles

  • Students: Search for tutors, book sessions, and provide feedback to improve the learning experience.
  • Tutors: Manage schedules, accept or reject bookings, and conduct tutoring sessions to help students succeed.
  • Admins: Oversee platform activities, manage users, and ensure quality control for a seamless experience.

Booking & Scheduling System

  • View tutor availability and book sessions at your convenience.
  • Tutors can accept or reject bookings based on their schedule.
  • Automated email and SMS reminders for upcoming sessions to keep everyone on track.

Tutor Verification & Quality Control

  • Tutors are required to provide academic proof or pass qualification tests to ensure expertise.
  • A student rating and review system to maintain transparency and trust.
  • Admin oversight to monitor tutor performance and ensure high-quality tutoring.

Our Mission

At Peer Tutoring, we believe in the power of collaborative learning. Our mission is to make education more accessible and personalized by connecting students with qualified peer tutors. We aim to foster academic excellence, build confidence, and create a supportive learning community where everyone can thrive.

Why Choose Peer Tutoring?

  • Personalized Learning: Tailored tutoring sessions to meet your unique academic needs.
  • Convenience: Easy scheduling and communication tools to save time and effort.
  • Trusted Tutors: Verified tutors with proven expertise to guide you effectively.
  • Collaborative Environment: A platform that encourages knowledge sharing and mutual growth.
